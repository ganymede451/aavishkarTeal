RoboWar

Event Description

Robo War where robots are built to fight against each other in an arena. The goal of the competition is for a robot to defeat all other robots in the arena, and the last robot standing is declared the winner. Prize pool: 50K

Rules

  • The maximum weight of the robot including batteries must not exceed 15 kg and the maximum allowable voltage is 24 volts.
  • Robot dimensions must be within 50cm x 50cm x 50cm.
  • The use of heat, fire and water as weapons is strictly prohibited.
  • Weapons designed to inflict invisible damage to robots using electricity, RF interference.
  • Electromagnetic fields, etc. are not permitted.
  • After registration, each team must bring the BOT to the university for calibration on the designated day.
  • Teams must build and bring in well-designed, radio-controlled robots that can push, throw, and flip opponents robots.
  • The following section contains all the rules and regulations regarding robots and the arena in which the tournament is held.
  • Event administrators reserve the right to cancel registration at any time if they determine that you have not followed the rules.
