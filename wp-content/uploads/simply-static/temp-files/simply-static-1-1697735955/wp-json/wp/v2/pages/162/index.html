Event Description<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Idea Based Competition will bring individuals from different fields to the table acting as a cabinet of ministers who will draft solutions via mutual discussions. The program has been designed to guide the table through issues, discussion and decision-making. Participants do not need prior preparation and can enjoy the event will a free mind.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Rules<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • Participants must bring their phones. (One can get their charger to the event as well)<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Each Participant will be provided with an ID containing their name, QR to LinkedIn and their secret Code at the back.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • The Volunteers will assign a situation to the table around which participants will discuss.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • There will be a screen with a QR code. Scanning the QR, Participants need to submit a prevailing issue that they think the table should discuss.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • The table will discuss and vote to choose 2-3 issues for further discussion.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Now, participants will again scan QR and submit solutions relevant to the issues.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Participants are encouraged to present arguments, criticize and defend arguments during the discussion.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Scores will be given based on the engagement of participants and the effectiveness of arguments<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • In between the session, participants will be asked to score each other’s performance.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Volunteers ill keep track of participants’ engagement for scoring.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Statistical methods and peer reviews will be used to optimize scoring.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n
