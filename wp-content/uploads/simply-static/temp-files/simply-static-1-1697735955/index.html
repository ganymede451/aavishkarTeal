Our vison

Aavhiskar aims to create a world ignited by the flames of innovation and creativity. We strive to cultivate a society where every individual is empowered to turn their ideas into impactful solutions, driving positive change and propelling us towards a brighter and technologically advanced tomorrow. We foster a culture of knowledge sharing and empowering students.

Aavishkar’24

Aavishkar, launched by KURC in 2017, is an annual robotics festival promoting Nepali youth in robotics through workshops, exhibitions, and competitions. Embrace a day filled with cutting-edge advancements, inspiring ideas, and a vibrant blend of technology. Witness the future unfold as we celebrate and showcase the essence of innovation in its various forms.

Our Goals

Aavishkar’s goal is to ignite innovation and passion in robotics. We aspire to cultivate a community of dedicated students, fostering a passion for robotics. Our goal is to drive impactful research, encourage interdisciplinary collaboration, and equip future leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to shape a technologically advanced world.

Pioneering Transformative Technology

Our vision is to harness transformative technology, empowering individuals and organizations to address global challenges and foster a connected, inclusive world. We strive for technological advancements that positively impact lives and drive a sustainable, prosperous future for all.

Igniting Innovation in Robotics

Aavishkar, initiated in 2017 by Kathmandu University Robotics Club (KURC), is an annual robotics festival igniting creativity and talent. The event motivates Nepal’s youth to excel in robotics, offering a global platform for innovation and education through various engaging activities and competitions.

Empowering Robotics Enthusiasts

Aavishkar aims to empower aspiring creators, students, and technology enthusiasts by showcasing their projects and fostering a passion for robotics. This festival, featuring diverse themes and competitions, is a dynamic hub that connects participants worldwide, promoting innovation and education in the exciting realm of robotics.
