Event Description<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

A cleaning robot competition is competition under the event AAVISHKAR 23. The main motive of this competition is to clean the waste present in any water sources like lakes or rivers. Robots participating in this competition have to clear the waste in the form of slits from the surface of water. These two robots will be competing with each other to collect the slits and reach the opponent base by crossing through different checkpoints. This event is the legacy of Aavishkar, so it is organized in every Aavishkar. Prize pool: 30K<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Rules<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • The Robots can be controlled through wireless remote only.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • The use of ready made toys is not allowed but one can use modules or circuits.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Power supply should be on board only and the electric voltage between 2 points anywhere in the machine should not be more than 24V DC at any point of time.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Participants are allowed only to make (n-2) restarts during the game where n stands for number of checkpoints.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • The name of the team must be a unique one with a team leader specified and only team leader can operate the robot.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • In case of any problem in the robot the game will not be replayed.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • No more AC\/DC power supply will be provided at the site of play.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Organizers reserve the right to ask for the explanation of the robot. The Organizers can change the rules of the game depending on the situation.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Positions of obstacles will be decided by the organizers on the particular day of the event.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • A team, an individual or a robot that violates the code of conduct can be disqualified from the tournament.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • The number of checkpoints the robot has to cross may vary and it is made known before the game starts.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • The organizers reserve the right to change any of the rules if necessary and will inform to the participants if any rules are changed via Contact number or email.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n
