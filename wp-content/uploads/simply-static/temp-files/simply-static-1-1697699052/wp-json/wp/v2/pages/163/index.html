Event Description<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

A hardware hackathon is a meeting of some of the most creative and tech-savvy minds and a breeding ground for innovation, as participants collaborate to try and create something entirely new and unique as quickly as possible. Groups will design and create a new prototype from scratch, using only a collection of provided materials. Prize pool: 25K<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Rules<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
    \n
  • The event will be conducted for two day.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Every team contain maximum 3 members.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Teams should make their project onsite.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Once the time of hackathon stop, all the team should stop their project.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Winner will be declared by judges via different judging criteria. The decision made by the judges will be final.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • After the completion of hackathon, each participating team should display their project to the public.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
