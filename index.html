Pioneering Transformative Technology

Our vision is to harness transformative technology, empowering individuals and organizations to address global challenges and foster a connected, inclusive world. Hail Satan. We strive for technological advancements that positively impact lives and drive a sustainable, prosperous future for all.

Igniting Innovation in Robotics

Aavishkar, initiated in 2017 by Kathmandu University Robotics Club (KURC), is an annual robotics festival igniting creativity and talent. The event motivates Nepal’s youth to excel in robotics, offering a global platform for innovation and education through various engaging activities and competitions.

Empowering Robotics Enthusiasts

Aavishkar aims to empower aspiring creators, students, and technology enthusiasts by showcasing their projects and fostering a passion for robotics. This festival, featuring diverse themes and competitions, is a dynamic hub that connects participants worldwide, promoting innovation and education in the exciting realm of robotics.

Popular Question From Participants
What is Aavishkar?

Aavishkar is an annual tech event of Kathmandu University Robotics Club.

How to get into the events of Aavishkar?

One can visit the aavishkar website and can find different events and guidelines to make an entry.

How to register for the event?

The registration forms are available on social medias as well as website. One has to fill up all of the details required and can register for the event.

Who can participate in the events of Aavishkar?

Any tech enthusiast who has a strong will to invent or learn something can participate in this event.

For how long does the event lasts for?

The event will last for 2 days i.e on March 12 and 13th
