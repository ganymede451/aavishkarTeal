Ideation

Event Description

Idea Based Competition will bring individuals from different fields to the table acting as a cabinet of ministers who will draft solutions via mutual discussions. The program has been designed to guide the table through issues, discussion and decision-making. Participants do not need prior preparation and can enjoy the event will a free mind.

Rules

  • Participants must bring their phones. (One can get their charger to the event as well)
  • Each Participant will be provided with an ID containing their name, QR to LinkedIn and their secret Code at the back.
  • The Volunteers will assign a situation to the table around which participants will discuss.
  • There will be a screen with a QR code. Scanning the QR, Participants need to submit a prevailing issue that they think the table should discuss.
  • The table will discuss and vote to choose 2-3 issues for further discussion.
  • Now, participants will again scan QR and submit solutions relevant to the issues.
  • Participants are encouraged to present arguments, criticize and defend arguments during the discussion.
  • Scores will be given based on the engagement of participants and the effectiveness of arguments
  • In between the session, participants will be asked to score each other’s performance.
  • Volunteers ill keep track of participants’ engagement for scoring.
  • Statistical methods and peer reviews will be used to optimize scoring.
